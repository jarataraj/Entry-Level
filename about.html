Comic #0: aboutme

Hello friend! My name is Emily, and I am the artist behind Entry-Level.

I created this comic because I have always loved drawing and cartoons. Now that I’m in the early stages of a “real-world” career, I figured that I could use that beloved, creative outlet as a means of expressing the rage and frustration I have felt while working an adult job.

One question I often get about my comics is, “Did those things really happen to you at work?” The answer to that question is yes and no. Most of my comics are based on real events and thoughts I have while I’m on the job, however I often add some embellishments for the sake of the punch line.

I hope you enjoy these comics! They are a work in progress just like my career, and I would love to hear your feedback. If you vibe with a comic or have some advice on how to make it better, leave a comment for me through any of my social media outlets. And please, share them with the world!
