Comic #0: aboutme

Hello friend! My name is Emily, and I am the artist behind Entry Level.

I created this comic to express the embarrasment, frustration, and occasional rage I have felt as a young adult working an "adult" job.

I graduated college and entered the "real" world about a year ago, and I currently work as an entry level environmental engineer.

Outside of work, I love to bike, hike, climb, and work on art. I really enjoy working with pastels.

I hope you enjoy these comics! They are a work in progress just like my career, and I would love to hear your feedback. If you vibe with a comic or have suggestions for improvement, leave a comment for me through any of the above social media outlets. And please, share them with the world!
